Our Mission

At Mango Byte, we come to work everyday to solve most significant dilemma in the contemporary field of technology: the relationship between business and user.


Publishers lack knowledge of what app they need. They do not know how to monetise their data or how to create substantial investment in technology. Advertisers do not know where their target users are, let alone how to reach them. Investors cannot stay with the current trends and therefore fail to recognize where users are spending most of their time, and most importantly, money.


For as long as we lived, data has been used to make informed decisions for businesses. Constituting this data are the users, the buyers of products, the consumers, the integers in a spreadsheets; not aware of the value they hold for companies. Our goal at Mango Byte is to make hte app ecosystem more transparent. We want the users to keep equal importance as that of investors, to be interdependent.
